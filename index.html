Exploring Volumes of Solids of Revolution

How to Use

  • Select an example from the dropdown (top right)
  • Show Region highlights the 2D area being rotated
  • Show Dimensions displays the sample rectangle with labeled r, h, Δx
  • Animate Rotation shows the rectangle rotating to form a slice
  • Show Sample Slice displays the 3D slice created by that rectangle
  • Show Slices displays all slices (Riemann sum)
  • Play Animation animates slices appearing one by one
  • Show Solid displays the full solid of revolution
  • Click "How is this integral formed?" for detailed breakdown

3D Controls

  • Drag to rotate view
  • Scroll to zoom
  • Right-drag to pan
